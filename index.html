Our Story

From Exotic Limes to Pure Juice

The Limeys story starts with exotic limes. Years ago, we began by offering fresh limes to restaurants and bars in Cape Town, South Africa.

We discovered that many establishments were struggling to extract maximum juice from the limes while maintaining quality and consistency. This led us to develop our unique single-strength process that produces 100% pure fruit juice without heating or pasteurising, ensuring all vitamins and natural goodness remain intact.
